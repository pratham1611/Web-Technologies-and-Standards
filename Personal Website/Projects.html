PROJECTS


SOCCER PLAYER PREDICTOR


Developed a model that predicts the type of soccer player based on user input. Worked on a dataset with player attributes like
Pace, Shot, Passing, Dribbling etc. Used K-Nearest Neighbors classification to find the K-closest points of a particular sample point.
The application returned the player role i.e Striker/Centre Back/Centre Midfield/Right Midfeld etc. as an output to user.

HYDROPONIC CONTROLLER


Designed a system for growing plants without soil, using mineral nutrient solutions. DHT11, float switches, pH sensors and
pressure valves were programmed in Arduino to control temperature, humidity, water level and provide corrective action.

BLACK JACK
